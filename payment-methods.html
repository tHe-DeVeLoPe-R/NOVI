Payment Security

How We Protect Your Information

  • SSL Encryption: All payment data is transmitted using 256-bit SSL encryption
  • PCI Compliance: We are PCI DSS Level 1 compliant, the highest level of payment security
  • Tokenization: Card details are tokenized and never stored on our servers
  • Fraud Detection: Advanced fraud detection systems monitor all transactions
  • 3D Secure: Additional authentication for supported cards

Your Responsibilities

  • Ensure your billing information is accurate and up-to-date
  • Keep your account credentials confidential
  • Report any unauthorized charges immediately
  • Verify your order confirmation and receipt

Payment Processing

When Are You Charged?

Your payment method will be charged when your order is confirmed and processed. For pre-order items, payment is collected at the time of order placement, not at shipment.

Payment Authorization

When you place an order, we may place an authorization hold on your payment method. This hold will be converted to a charge once your order ships. If your order is cancelled, the hold will be released within 5-7 business days.

Failed Payments

If your payment fails, we will:

  • Send you an email notification
  • Hold your order for 48 hours
  • Allow you to update your payment information
  • Cancel the order if payment is not received within 48 hours

Currency

All prices are displayed in USD. If you're paying with a card issued in a different currency, your bank will convert the amount at their current exchange rate. Please note that your bank may charge a foreign transaction fee.

Billing & Invoices

Order Confirmation

You will receive an order confirmation email immediately after placing your order. This email serves as your receipt and includes:

  • Order number and date
  • Itemized list of products
  • Pricing and tax breakdown
  • Shipping address and method
  • Payment method used

Tax Information

Sales tax is calculated based on your shipping address and applicable state and local tax rates. Tax amounts are displayed at checkout before you complete your purchase.

Payment FAQs

Can I use multiple payment methods for one order?

Currently, we only accept one payment method per order. If you'd like to split payment, consider using a digital wallet or buy now, pay later option.

Do you accept international credit cards?

Yes, we accept credit cards from most countries. However, the billing address must match the address on file with your card issuer.

Is it safe to save my payment information?

Yes, it's completely safe. We use tokenization to securely store your payment information, which means we never have access to your actual card details.
