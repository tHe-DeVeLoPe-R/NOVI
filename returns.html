Returns & Exchanges

Hassle-free returns within 30 days

Our Return Policy

We want you to love your NOVI jewelry. If you're not completely satisfied, we offer a 30-day return policy for unworn, undamaged items in their original packaging.

Eligible for Return

  • Items purchased within the last 30 days
  • Unworn and in original condition
  • Original packaging and tags intact
  • Proof of purchase (order confirmation or receipt)

Not Eligible for Return

  • Custom or personalized jewelry
  • Earrings (for hygiene reasons)
  • Items on final sale or clearance
  • Items damaged due to misuse
  • Items purchased more than 30 days ago

How to Return an Item

  1. Log into your account and go to "Order History"
  2. Select the item you wish to return
  3. Click "Request Return" and select your reason
  4. Print the prepaid return label sent to your email
  5. Pack the item securely in its original packaging
  6. Drop off at any carrier location or schedule a pickup

Return Shipping

We provide free return shipping for all domestic returns. Simply use the prepaid label we email you. For international returns, customers are responsible for return shipping costs.

Exchanges

Need a different size or style? We offer free exchanges!

How to Exchange

  1. Follow the return process above
  2. Select "Exchange" and choose your replacement item
  3. We'll ship your new item immediately upon receiving your return

Exchange Benefits

  • Free shipping on exchange items
  • Priority processing for exchanges
  • No restocking fees
  • Price adjustments if the new item costs less

Refunds

Refund Processing

Once we receive your return, we'll inspect it and process your refund within 5-7 business days. You'll receive an email confirmation when your refund is issued.

Refund Method

Refunds are issued to the original payment method:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-10 business days
  • PayPal: 3-5 business days
  • Digital Wallets: 3-5 business days

Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may be issued for:

  • Items not in original condition
  • Items with missing tags or packaging
  • Items returned after 30 days (at our discretion)

Gift Returns

Received a gift that's not quite right? No problem!

  • Gift recipients can return items for store credit
  • Original purchaser can return for refund to original payment method
  • Gift receipts hide pricing information
  • Same 30-day return window applies

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item:

  1. Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  2. Provide photos of the damage or defect
  3. We'll send a replacement immediately
  4. No need to return the damaged item

Our Quality Guarantee

All NOVI jewelry is inspected before shipping. If you discover a manufacturing defect within 1 year of purchase, we'll repair or replace it free of charge.

Return FAQs

How long do I have to return an item?

You have 30 days from the delivery date to initiate a return.

Can I return sale items?

Yes, sale items can be returned unless marked as "Final Sale" or "Clearance."

What if I lost my receipt?

No problem! We can look up your order using your email address or order number.
